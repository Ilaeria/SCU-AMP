Dislikes and Stressors

Dislikes

I don't like physical contact because it makes me feel uncomfortable and I am just not used to it in social situations.

You can help me with this by refraining from unnecessary physical contact, specifically not hugging me to say hello or goodbye, or patting me on the shoulder or back.

Stressors

I can become stressed or anxious if I am excluded from conversations for long periods of time. Sometimes it might seem like I'm quiet or disinterested, but that's only because of my narrow interests, which prevents me from having anything interesting to say with regards to mainstream interests.

You can help me with this by trying to include me and not assuming I'm disinterested if I seem aloof. Also, just asking me simple questions to get my opinion often helps.
